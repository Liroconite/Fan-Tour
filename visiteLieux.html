Must-See Places For Fans

in New Zealand

Hobbiton Village, Matamata

Peaceful and green surroundings, this village represents the village of the hobbits in the film "Lord of the Rings", moreover the village is one of the only unnatural landscapes still in place which makes it an ideal place for fans to visit. After a two-hour guided tour, a homemade beer is offered at the Dragon Vert tavern.

Tongariro, National Park

Tongariro National Park is a very large park of 796 km2, it is on Mount Ngauruhoe that Frodo and Sam cross it and then climb it, to destroy the ring by throwing it into its crater. To follow their adventure, you can hike (alpine crossing) to the summit or even hike to visit this park which is full of unforgettable places.

city of Wellington

Mount Victoria

Lookout Road, Hataitai, Wellington 6021, New Zealand

Mount Victoria Park is located in the heart of downtown Wellington. It is easily accessible by car, on foot or by public transportation. The view of Wellington Bay is well worth a visit. It is in the Mount Victoria Forest Park that Frodo and 3 Hobbits narrowly escape the Black Rider hidden beneath the roots.

Weta Caves

1 Weka Street, Miramar, Wellington 6022, New Zealand

Just 21 minutes from Mount Victoria is a studio called Weta Caves. It is in this studio that the costumes, sets, special effects and make-up were designed. Through the guided tour you will find out how the world of the Lord of the Rings was made, not to be missed.

Restaurant

O-Del-Emz

33 Arawa Street, Matamata 3400, New Zealand
O Del Emz Cafe has been an outstanding local cafe for seven years now. We pride ourselves on consistently providing the best coffee and food in town, all of which we make fresh in our uniquely small kitchen. To see how we began just take a look through our photo history book on display in the cafe. We love what we do and know our customers love it as well. Our staff are friendly and helpful. We love to go the extra mile for our customers and will cater to any dietary need. We also offer an out-catering service which has proven to be very popular. We can also cater for dinners in the cafe by arrangement. The owner Derek has over 20 years experience in hospitality and is a superb chef. He personally trains all his staff to his high standards. As a long-standing local cafe, we believe in giving back to our community by supporting local schools and sports clubs as well as the Waikato Westpac Rescue Helicopter. O Del Emz: Bringing colour to our town corner.

Osteria Matamata

59 Broadway Matamata
The Osteria group is a family-run restaurant group, with several locations throughout New Zealand including Matamata, Mount Maunganui & Ohakune. An osteria in Italy originated in 13th century Venice & was originally a place which served wine and simple food. The name evokes the hospitality of ancient local inns which date back to Roman times, a place to eat, drink and be merry. Our modern-day osterias retain this ambience but have evolved over the years to be more sophisticated venues for food as well as serving more varieties of wine.
